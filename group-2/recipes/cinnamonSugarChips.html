Cinnamon Sugar Tortilla Chips

Ingredients:

  • Flour tortillas
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Brush tortillas with melted butter, then sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
  2. Cut the tortillas into triangles and bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 10 minutes until crispy.
  3. Let them cool and enjoy!
