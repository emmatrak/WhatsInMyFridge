Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • one chicken breast (cooked)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables(optional)
  • half of an onion (optional)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 refrigerated pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and cook onions until soft, if you have onions.
  3. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, then gradually add chicken broth and milk, stirring until thickened.
  4. Add shredded chicken, vegetables, salt, and pepper, mixing well.
  5. Place the pie crust into a baking dish, pour the mixture in, and top with another layer of crust.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
