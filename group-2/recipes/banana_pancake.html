Banana Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 Very Ripe Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Thoroughly mash the banana(s) in a bowl
  2. Crack both eggs into the bowl with the bananas
  3. Mix; the batter should be very loose
  4. Heat up pan to medium heat on a stove top or griddle; melt butter onto the pan
  5. Pour batter into the pan; make small pancakes because they will be fragile and hard to flip
  6. Flip after 1 minute or when golden brown
  7. Cook other side for 1 minute or until golden brown
  8. Serve plain or with any desired toppings
