Peanut Butter & Chocolate Rice Krispies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup honey

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, melt peanut butter and honey together in the microwave.
  2. Stir in Rice Krispies and mix until well-coated.
  3. Melt chocolate chips in the microwave and drizzle over the top.
  4. Let it cool and set before cutting into squares.
