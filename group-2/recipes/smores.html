S'mores in a Mug

Ingredients:

  • 1 graham cracker
  • 2 marshmallows
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Break graham cracker into pieces and place at the bottom of a mug.
  2. Add marshmallows and chocolate chips on top.
  3. Microwave for 30 seconds to 1 minute until marshmallows puff up.
