Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Bread; 2 slices
  • Cheese; Enough to cover at least one slice of the bread
  • Butter or any butter substitue; Mayo, oil, margarine, bacon fat, etc.
  • Optional: some type of meat that can be eaten partially raw or is precooked

Steps:

  1. Take one of your pieces of bread and butter one of its open faces
  2. Lay your cheese out on the side that is not buttered
  3. If you are going to add meat then add it now, on top of the cheese
  4. Put the other piece of bread on top of the meat or cheese
  5. Butter the outward facing side of the top slice of bread
  6. Set your pan to medium heat
  7. Cook one side of the bread until the cheese is melting and the bread is browning
  8. Flip and cook the other side
  9. Eat while hot
