Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic in a little olive oil.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry with a splash of soy sauce.
  3. Serve with brown rice or quinoa.

Calories:

About 200–250 per serving (without rice).
