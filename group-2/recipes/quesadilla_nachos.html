Quesadilla Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 2 Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • any other nacho toppings you want

Steps:

  1. Lay your tortilla out flat and put your cheese on top
  2. Put your other tortilla on top
  3. Microwave until cheese is melted
  4. Cut quesadilla into chip shaped pieces
  5. Bake or air fry until crispy
  6. Serve with desired nacho toppings
