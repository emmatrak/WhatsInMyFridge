Microwave Mug Brownie

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix all ingredients.
  2. Microwave for 1-2 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Let it cool for a few minutes, then enjoy your warm, gooey brownie!
