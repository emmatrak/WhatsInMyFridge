Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • Big handful of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Boil some water and cook the pasta and dehydrated veggies in it.
  2. Drain the water and set the pasta aside.
  3. Add milk into the pan and set to low heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Add your cheese and seasoning packets, stirring until fully incorporated.
  5. Add your butter into the pot now if you are using it.
  6. Add your noodles back in and stir/mix.
  7. Ready to eat!
