Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Chicken breast (or canned chicken)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Dice the chicken breast (or use canned chicken) and cook in a skillet with a little olive oil until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add frozen mixed veggies and stir-fry with garlic and a splash of soy sauce until veggies are heated through.
  3. Serve as is or over a bed of brown rice or quinoa for added fullness (optional).
