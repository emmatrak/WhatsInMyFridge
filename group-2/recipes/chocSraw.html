Chocolate-Dipped Frozen Strawberries

Ingredients:

  • 10-15 strawberries
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Slice strawberries into 1-inch pieces.
  2. Melt chocolate chips in the microwave.
  3. Dip strawberry pieces into the chocolate and freeze them on a tray for at least 2 hours.
