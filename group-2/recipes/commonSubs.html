Common Recipe Substitutions

1. Butter

Substitute with:

  • Margarine
  • Applesauce (for a healthier alternative)
  • Greek yogurt (for a creamy texture)
  • Vegetable oil (in some baked goods)

2. Eggs

Substitute with:

  • Mashed banana (1/4 cup for each egg)
  • Applesauce (1/4 cup for each egg)
  • Chia seeds (1 tbsp chia seeds mixed with 3 tbsp water = 1 egg)
  • Flaxseed meal (1 tbsp flaxseed meal + 3 tbsp water = 1 egg)

3. Milk

Substitute with:

  • Almond milk
  • Soy milk
  • Oat milk
  • Rice milk
  • Coconut milk (for a richer taste)

4. Sugar

Substitute with:

  • Honey (1 cup sugar = 3/4 cup honey)
  • Maple syrup (1 cup sugar = 3/4 cup maple syrup)
  • Stevia (a natural zero-calorie sweetener)
  • Coconut sugar (a healthier alternative)

5. Flour

Substitute with:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Almond flour (for gluten-free baking)
  • Oat flour (for a nutty flavor)
  • Coconut flour (for gluten-free and lower-carb recipes)

6. Heavy Cream

Substitute with:

  • Half and half
  • Milk and cornstarch (for a lighter option)
  • Coconut cream (for dairy-free and rich texture)

7. Buttermilk

Substitute with:

  • Milk with a splash of lemon juice or vinegar (1 cup milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice/vinegar)
  • Plain yogurt (for a thicker texture)
  • Coconut milk with a splash of vinegar (for dairy-free options)

8. Oil

Substitute with:

  • Applesauce (for a lower-fat option)
  • Yogurt (for a creamy texture)
  • Coconut oil (for a slightly different flavor)
