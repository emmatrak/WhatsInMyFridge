Honey Butter Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Bread; white or wheat, four slices
  • Honey; at least 1.5 tablespoons or about 1/8 of those honey bear containers; could use molasses or maple syrup as a substitute
  • Sugar; 3.5 tables; brown if you have it, white will also work, could even use cinnamon sugar
  • half a stick of butter; can be salted or unsalted; should be room temp if you can

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees(F) or preheat air fryer to 330 degrees(F)
  2. Mix butter, sugar, and honey in a bowl. Add a little salt if you use unsalted butter
  3. If you do not want crust the cut them off now
  4. Spread your mixture on all faces of the bread, including the crust if you left it on
  5. Put into the oven/air fryer on some parchment paper and cook for 8-10 minutes
  6. Take out and flip over then cook for another 8-10 minutes or until golden brown and crispy
  7. Let cool for a little bit and then eat
