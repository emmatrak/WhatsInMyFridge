Lentil and Veggie Soup

Ingredients:

  • Canned lentils (or dried lentils)
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Spinach (optional)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat a little olive oil and sauté chopped onions, garlic, carrots, and celery until softened.
  2. Add canned lentils (drained and rinsed) and canned diced tomatoes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth (enough to cover the mixture) and bring to a simmer.
  4. Let it cook for 20–30 minutes, adding spinach towards the end if desired.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and any herbs you like (thyme, oregano, or cumin work well).

Calories:

About 200–250 per serving.
