Tuna Salad Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna
  • Greek yogurt (instead of mayo)
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. Mix tuna with Greek yogurt and a little mustard.
  2. Scoop the mixture onto large lettuce leaves to make wraps.

Calories:

About 200 per serving (2 wraps).
