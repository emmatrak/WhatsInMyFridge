Instant Ramen Upgrade

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack of instant ramen
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon of mayo

Steps:

  1. Boil one cup of water
  2. Add noodles and dehydrated veggies from the ramen pack to the water and let cook
  3. While the noodles are cooking, mix the egg yolk, mayo, and ramen seasoning in a bowl until a smooth paste
  4. Pour the pasta water into the bowl with the egg/mayo/seasoning mixture and mix until fully incorporated
  5. add the noodles into the browl and eat

Optional Add-ons:

  • Add chopped green onion and chili crisp to the final product
  • Add a pinch of MSG or a splash of soy sauce into the broth while you cook the noodles
  • Add anything else you would add into ramen
